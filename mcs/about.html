About Us

Melbourne Cardiology Services started with a conviction to focus on quality, integrity, and excellence in a field where profits and growth are increasingly becoming dominating factors and primary motivation. Here at MCS, we don't seek to do more in the same timeframe, or cram as many patients as possible in a day. Our primary focus is on the quality of the tests performed and reported. We ensure the integrity and quality of the tests and reports by having an eye for detail, not rushing, but spending time as necessary to obtain as much information as possible and time to analyse every image acquired.

Together with an attitude of excellence, we aim to offer a good experience and outcome to patients, as well as reports to referring doctors that are accurate and trustworthy. In keeping with our tenets of quality, integrity, and excellence, our doctors and sonographers are well experienced with many years in the field.

At MCS, we strive to be more personal and relational in our approach, where patients won't be treated like a number or dollar sign, compared to the larger and more "corporate" feel of some other practices. MCS may be a relatively new practice, but we certainly are not new to the industry. If you are seeking a patient-focused experience, where quality, integrity, and excellence are at the core of what we do, then you've come to the right place.

We endeavour to keep our waitlists low and reports will be sent in a timely manner.

Kenny Lee

Kenny Lee

AMS

Cardiac Sonographer

Manager

Kenny is a very experienced and all-rounded cardiac sonographer and cardiac technologist. He has been scanning for over 25 years wth vast experience with both paediatric and adult patients.

Kenny was initially trained in paediatric echocardiology by the prominent Dr. T H Goh, and was exposed to a myriad of congenital heart disease, including some experience with premature babies. He was also among one of the few techs at that time that was also trained in adult echocardiography concurrently. His training also included Holters, ECGs, stress testing, and cath lab monitoring.

Kenny has a keen interest in the area of stress echocardiography and quality assurance in the echo lab. He has continued to maintain a busy load with transthoracic echocardiograms, and has been involved in training and mentoring trainee cardiac sonographers. He is a highly competent cardiac sonographer with strong emphasis on workplace excellence, integrity, and quality.

Larry Ponnuthurai

Larry Ponnuthurai

MBBCh FRACP FCSANZ DDU

Deputy Director of Cardiology - Northern Health

Head of Cathlab - Northern Health

Head of Coronary Care Unit - Northern Health

Cardiac Imaging (Echo & Cardiac CT) Specialist

Dr F A (Larry) Ponnuthurai is currently the Deputy Director of Cardiology at Northern Hospital as well as the head of the Hospital’s Cardiac Cathlab and Coronary Care Unit.

Larry completed his Specialist training in Cardiology at the Alfred Hospital in Melbourne. He has expertise in two cardiac subspecialties including a Fellowship in Cardiac imaging (echocardiography) at Monash Hospital and the Austin Hospital and a second Fellowship in Interventional Cardiology at the John Radcliffe Hospital in Oxford, United Kingdom.

His research includes published articles in the fields of Interventional Cardiology, echocardiography and non-coronary intervention. He can offer patients a comprehensive cardiac care which includes:

  • Interventional Cardiology (coronary artery blockages treated by balloons and stents)
  • Cardiac Imaging (transthoracic echocardiograms and transoesophageal echocardiograms)
  • Heart failure (management of cardiomyopathy and valvular heart disease)
  • Cardiac Risk Assessment (ECG stress tests, stress echo tests, CT coronary angiography)

His role as Deputy Director includes the education of Cardiology trainee doctors as well as Medical students from the University of Melbourne. He regularly participates in General Practitioner education programs.

He is involved in both public and private practice. H1e consults at Melbourne Heart Institute (Moonee Ponds), the Epworth Hospital (Richmond), The Cardiologists (Epping), as well as Epping Specialist Centre – Northern Heart (Epping). He performs coronary angioplasty and stenting at the Northern Hospital and Epworth Hospital.

Larry’s priority will always remain his patients. He is well known for his careful and meticulous approach to his cardiological procedures to achieve the optimal outcome and wellbeing for his patients. Patients invariably comment on his approachable manner and appreciate the time he takes to listen to their symptoms, concerns and fears.

“I have been a Medical Doctor for over 20 years and am still very passionate about delivering a personalised and balanced cardiac management approach to each of my patients. I feel strongly that my patients’ wishes should be respected and their fears should be appreciated and allayed before their medical treatment can be considered a success. Most importantly, I believe it is an honour and a privilege to serve my community with compassion, humility, integrity and to the absolute best of my ability.”
