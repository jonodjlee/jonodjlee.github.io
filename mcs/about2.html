Melbourne Cardiology Services started with a conviction to focus on quality, integrity, and excellence in a field where profits and growth are increasingly becoming dominating factors and primary motivation. Here at MCS, we don't seek to do more in the same timeframe, or cram as many patients as possible in a day. Our primary focus is on the quality of the tests performed and reported. We ensure the integrity and quality of the tests and reports by having an eye for detail, not rushing, but spending time as necessary to obtain as much information as possible and time to analyse every image acquired.

Together with an attitude of excellence, we aim to offer a good experience and outcome to patients, as well as reports to referring doctors that are accurate and trustworthy. In keeping with our motto of quality, integrity, and excellence, our doctors and sonographers are well experienced with many years in the field.

At MCS, we strive to be more personal and relational in our approach, where patients won't be treated like a number or dollar sign, compared to the larger and more "corporate" feel of some other practices. MCS may be a new practice, but we certainly are not new to the industry. If you are seeking a patient-focused experience, where quality, integrity, and excellence are at the core of what we do, then you've come to the right place.

Our endeavour is to keep our wait lists low and reports will be sent in a timely manner.








Larry Ponnuthurai

MBBCh, FRACP, FCSANZ, DDU

Deputy Director of Cardiology - Northern Health
Head of Cathlab - Northern Health
Head of Coronary Care Unit - Northern Health
Cardiac Imaging (Echo & Cardiac CT) Specialist


Interventional Cardiologist

Dr F A (Larry) Ponnuthurai is currently the Deputy Director of Cardiology at Northern Hospital as well as the head of the Hospital’s Cardiac Cathlab and Coronary Care Unit.

Larry Ponnuthurai completed his Specialist training in Cardiology at the Alfred Hospital in Melbourne. He has expertise in two cardiac subspecialties including a Fellowship in Cardiac imaging (echocardiography) at Monash Hospital and the Austin Hospital and a second Fellowship in Interventional Cardiology at the John Radcliffe Hospital in Oxford, United Kingdom.

His research includes published articles in the fields of Interventional Cardiology, echocardiography and non-coronary intervention. He can offer patients a comprehensive cardiac care which includes:

  • Interventional Cardiology (coronary artery blockages treated by balloons and stents)
  • Cardiac Imaging (echocardiograms and transoesophageal echocardiograms)
  • Heart failure (management of cardiomyopathy and valvular heart disease)
  • Cardiac Risk Assessment (ECG stress tests, Stress echo tests)

His role as Deputy Director includes the education of Cardiology trainee doctors as well as Medical students from the University of Melbourne. He regularly participates in General Practitioner education programs.

He is involved in both public and private practice where he consults at the Melbourne Heart Institute (Moonee Ponds), the Epworth Hospital (Richmond) as well as Epping Specialist Centre – Northern Heart (Epping). He performs coronary angioplasty and stenting at the Northern Hospital, Epworth Hospital and Western Private Hospital.

“I have been a Medical Doctor for over 20 years and am still very passionate about delivering a personalised and balanced cardiac management approach to each of my patients. I feel strongly that my patients’ wishes should be respected and their fears should be appreciated and allayed before their medical treatment can be considered a success. Most importantly, I believe it is an honour and a privilege to serve my community with compassion, humility, integrity and to the absolute best of my ability.”







Kenny Lee

AMS

Manager


Cardiac Sonographer

Kenny is a very experienced and all-rounded cardiac sonographer and cardiac technologist. He has been scanning for over 25 years wth experience in both paediatric and adult patients.

Kenny was initially trained in paediatric echocardiology by the prominent Dr. T H Goh, and was exposed to a myriad of congenital heart disease, including some experience with premature babies. He was also among one of the few techs at that time that was also trained in adult echocardiography concurrently. His training also included Holters, ECGs, stress testing, and cath lab monitoring.

In recent years, Kenny developed a keen interest in the area of stress echocardiography and have performed in excess of 11,000 stress echo studies in the last 5 years alone, whilst concurrently maintaining a busy load with transthoracic echocardiograms, as well as training and mentoring a new trainee cardiac sonographer.

Kenny is a highly competent cardiac sonographer with strong emphasis on workplace excellence, integrity, and quality.
